# Simple Interest Calculator

## Description

This project is a Simple Interest Calculator that computes the simple interest earned or paid on a principal amount over a specific period of time at a given annual interest rate.

## Formula Used

The calculator uses the following formula:

**Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100**

Where:

* **Principal (P)** = Initial amount of money
* **Rate (R)** = Annual interest rate (in percentage)
* **Time (T)** = Time period (in years)

## Example

Suppose:

* Principal = 10,000
* Rate = 5%
* Time = 2 years

Simple Interest = (10000 × 5 × 2) / 100

Simple Interest = 1,000

Therefore, the interest earned is **1,000**, and the total amount becomes **11,000**.

## Usage

1. Enter the principal amount.
2. Enter the annual interest rate.
3. Enter the time period in years.
4. Run the calculator.
5. The calculated simple interest will be displayed.

* Quick and accurate simple interest calculation
